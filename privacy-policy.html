Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Nova Workout respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

Information We Collect

We may collect information you provide directly, such as account details, workout entries, goals, and support requests.

We may also collect limited technical information such as device type, app version, and basic usage data to improve app performance.

How We Use Information

We use information to provide app features, save your workout data, improve the app, respond to support requests, and maintain security.

Advertising

The app may display ads provided by third-party advertising partners such as Google AdMob. These partners may collect and process data according to their own privacy policies.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share limited information with service providers only when needed to operate, maintain, and improve the app.

Data Retention

We keep data only as long as necessary to provide the service, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements.

Your Choices

You can request account or data deletion using the instructions on our Delete Account page.

Children's Privacy

The app is not intended for children under the age required by local law without parental consent.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at support@novastore.co.za.
