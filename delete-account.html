Delete Account and Data

Last updated: 2026-02-26

Nova Workout And Gym Tracker is operated by NOVASTORE (PTY) LTD ("we", "us", "our").

This page explains how to request deletion of your Nova Workout And Gym Tracker account and associated data.

Option 1: Delete your account inside the app (recommended)

  1. Open Nova Workout And Gym Tracker
  2. Go to Settings
  3. Scroll to Danger Zone
  4. Tap Delete Account
  5. Confirm deletion

When you delete your account in the app, we delete your account and associated cloud data.

Option 2: Request deletion by email

If you cannot access the app, email us from the address linked to your account:

Email: support@novastore.co.za
Subject: Delete my Nova Workout And Gym Tracker account

Please include:

  • The email address used to sign in
  • Your display name, if you know it

What data is deleted

When your account deletion is completed, we delete:

  • Your account identifier (UID) and profile data stored for the app
  • Workout logs and training data stored in our cloud for your account
  • Streaks, goals, quests, preferences, and progression data stored for your account

What data may be kept

We may retain limited information only when required for legal, accounting, fraud prevention, or support purposes, such as:

  • Purchase or transaction records needed for tax, accounting, and dispute handling, retained for the legally required period
  • Minimal logs needed for security and abuse prevention, retained for a limited period

Payments are processed by Google Play or Apple App Store. We do not store full card details.

Retention period

  • Account and workout data: deleted after the request is verified and processed
  • Purchase records: retained only as required by law and accounting obligations
  • Security and diagnostic logs: retained for limited periods for reliability and security

Questions

If you have questions, contact support@novastore.co.za.
