Delete Account

If you would like to delete your Nova Workout account and associated data, email us at support@novastore.co.za using the subject line: Delete My Account.

Please include

Your account email address and any details that help us identify your account.

What gets deleted

We will delete your account data associated with Nova Workout, subject to any legal or operational retention requirements.

Retention

Some limited records may be retained where required for legal, security, fraud prevention, billing, or compliance purposes.

Processing time

We aim to process verified deletion requests within a reasonable time after confirming ownership of the account.

Contact

If you need help, contact support@novastore.co.za.
